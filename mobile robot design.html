Project Name

Autonomous Mobile Robot with Sheet Metal Fabrication

Objective

Design and fabricate a mobile robot chassis using sheet metal bending and welding techniques, integrating mechanical and electrical systems.

Description

  • Chassis Design: Fabricated from 1.5mm thick sheet metal using manual cutting and 90° bending.
  • Sheet Metal Work: Three main components welded together for structural integrity.
  • Damping System: Integrated vibration absorption for improved stability and performance.
  • Electrical Integration: Complete wiring and motor control systems.
  • Cover Design: Removable cover for easy access to internal components.
  • Cost Optimization: Focus on manufacturing efficiency and material selection.
